HOW TO PLAY
01
You have 20 minutes to manage a full ER shift. The clock is always running.
02
You receive a handover of 1–2 patients with pending results, plus a waiting list ranked by acuity.
03
For each patient: take history, perform physical exam, set your plan, order investigations, give interventions, then dispose.
04
Results arrive mid-shift as notifications. Act on critical findings or your patient suffers.
05
Miss the right diagnosis or critical intervention and the patient dies. Scores revealed only at the end.
06
Each patient is worth 8 points: Plan · Investigations · Interventions · Impression · Disposition.
